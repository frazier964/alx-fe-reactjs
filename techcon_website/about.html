Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of developers and tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has evolved into a global platform for innovation, drawing thousands of attendees from around the world.

Our Mission

TechCon aims to inspire, educate, and empower individuals through cutting-edge technology. Our goal is to foster collaboration, drive innovation, and highlight tech's role in improving lives worldwide.

Alexei Romanov – Pioneer in quantum encryption, Alexei has contributed to reshaping global cybersecurity standards.
